Mission

To support young atlhtes living in Chigago's nothside neighborhoods, who have an interest in learning and playing soccer,with oppurnaties to learn and practice skills related to the game of soccer, spefically those skills around team cooperation and good sportmanship.

Vision

The Northside Youth Soccer League aspire to develop strong, well-rounded, and mindful atletes through the building of character, self-discipline, and leadership.

General information

The Northside Youth League was establishshed in 1996 to provide athletes resinding in Chigago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play soccer. To be a member of NYSL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chigago northside neighborhood.NYSL is ran by a small full-time staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents.
